創作方式的變革

Revolution in Creation Methods

傳統模式： 需要數百人協作，制作周期往往長達數年，成本高昂。

Traditional Model: Requires hundreds of people to collaborate, production cycles often last for years, and costs are high.

AI原生模式： 一個創作者可能只需要幾台電腦、幾個AI工具，就能在幾週內完成一部高質量的作品。

AI-Native Model: A creator may only need a few computers and AI tools to complete a high-quality work within weeks.

案例：AI短片《The Last Painter》只有3個人的制作團隊，2個月完成， 視覺效果和敘事質量足以媲美傳統的百萬美元級別制作。

Case: AI short film "The Last Painter" had only 3 people in the production team, completed in 2 months, with visual effects and narrative quality comparable to traditional million-dollar productions.

Skill Module Value: Skills learned by AI during creation can be sold separately

Community Governance Value: Fans can participate in subsequent development decisions through tokens
